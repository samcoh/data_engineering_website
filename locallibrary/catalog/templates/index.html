About Us

The Michigan Daily Data Engineering Team is sub-sector of the Web Team. The main purpose of the team is to retrieve interesting datasets, perform analysis, and create visualizations. The visualizations that we create get published in the Michigan Daily Newspaper. Sometimes we collaborate with different sections to discuss our findings.
